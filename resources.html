360°Kids

Charity


Every kid deserves access to a safe home. 360°kids now serves over 2,500 youth each year, from every municipality in York Region. Although in the last three years we saw this number fluctuate, our commitment to providing essential housing, employment, education and well-being services, never wavered. 360°Kids aims to prevent homelessness by helping youth at risk or in crisis transition to a state of safety and stability.

Room for a Child

Charity


At Room for a Child we create beautiful bedrooms for children in need. We do that by accepting good, used furniture from individuals as well as corporate donations of items such as paint and linens. All work is done by volunteers.
